# LunaShade Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 20, 2026_

LunaShade is designed with privacy as a first-class principle.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** LunaShade does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
data, browsing history, page content, analytics, or telemetry.

## What we store

LunaShade stores only your preferences locally in your browser, using
Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync` API:

- Whether LunaShade is enabled
- Whether global mode is on
- Your brightness and contrast values
- Your list of excluded / allowed domains

If you are signed in to Chrome, this data syncs across your own devices via
Google's standard sync mechanism. We never see it.

## Permissions

- **storage** — to save your preferences.
- **activeTab** — to know which site the popup is acting on.
- **scripting** + host access — to apply the dark theme to the pages you visit.

No data leaves your browser.

For questions, please open an issue on the project repository.
