# Chrome Web Store Listing Copy

## Name
LunaShade — Elegant Dark Mode

## Short description (132 chars max)
A premium, lightweight dark mode for the web. Smart theming, per-site control, brightness & contrast — privacy-first and fast.

## Detailed description

LunaShade brings a beautiful, carefully tuned dark mode to every website you
visit — without the broken layouts, washed-out images, or jarring flashes
that plague typical dark-mode extensions.

✦ **Smart theming engine** — Preserves images, video, SVG and canvas. No more
ghostly pictures or inverted logos.

✦ **Per-site control** — Toggle LunaShade on or off for any site with one
click. Use Global mode to theme everything, or Allowlist mode to opt in
site-by-site.

✦ **Brightness & contrast** — Fine-tune the look to match your monitor and
your eyes. Live preview as you drag.

✦ **Premium design** — A glassmorphism-inspired popup with smooth
animations, polished typography, and a memorable visual identity.

✦ **Privacy first** — No accounts. No tracking. No telemetry. Your settings
sync via your own Chrome account; nothing is sent to us.

✦ **Lightweight & fast** — Vanilla JavaScript, Manifest V3, minimal
permissions, no external libraries. Optimized for large and dynamic sites.

Make the web easier on your eyes — with LunaShade.

## Category
Accessibility

## Permissions justification

- `storage` — Save your LunaShade preferences locally.
- `activeTab` — Show the current site in the popup and toggle it.
- `scripting` + `<all_urls>` — Apply the dark theme on the pages you visit.
